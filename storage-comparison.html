Your full requirements brief — bring this when you visit facilities or use it to screen by phone before making trips.

Non-negotiable
  • Climate controlled (interior, not just exterior building)
  • Stable temperature — art-safe environment
  • Ground floor or genuine freight elevator (rolling racks must move freely)
  • 10×15 minimum — 10×20 preferred
  • No Public Storage
  • Active access — not set-and-forget
Contents — Art
  • 3 large boxed canvases: ~9×9 ft, ~7×7 ft, ~4×5 ft
  • 1 rolled painting (large, lightweight)
  • 1 box (~3×2×4 ft) with smaller works
  • All professionally packed, wrapped in non-reactive plastic
  • Stored upright on runners, raised off floor, slight lean for airflow
  • Ceiling height must clear 9+ ft canvases stored upright
Contents — Working Archive
  • ~40 bins, labeled, stackable (stacked ~5 high)
  • Metro shelving units on locking casters
  • 2 professional rolling racks (actively used)
  • Backdrop tubes / fabric rolls
  • Steamer, small appliances (Sonos, blender)
  • Sewing machine
  • Herman Miller chair + folding table
Target Unit Layout
  • Back wall: canvases upright on runners, raised off floor
  • Side wall 1: bins stacked
  • Side wall 2: shelving + equipment
  • Front/corner: backdrops, fabric, steamer
  • Center: rolling racks + chair + table
  • Clear working aisle down the center — U-shape layout
Questions to Ask Facilities
  • Actual ceiling height in 10×15 / 10×20 units?
  • Ground floor available? If not, elevator — freight or standard?
  • Hallway width — can rolling racks move freely?
  • Access hours — evenings and weekends?
  • Climate control spec — temp range maintained?
  • Loading dock or drive-up? Is there a ramp?
  • Unit availability — any waitlist?
  • Month-to-month vs. long-term discount?
Decision Priority
  • 1. Climate stability (art protection)
  • 2. Layout usability (working archive, not just storage)
  • 3. Ease of access (racks, bins, repeated visits)
  • 4. Proximity to Blue Hill / parkways
  • 5. Unit availability (10×15 or 10×20)
  • 6. Local/regional operator preference
